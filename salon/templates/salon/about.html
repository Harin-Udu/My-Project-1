Our Story

Valencia Salon was born out of a simple belief: everyone deserves to feel confident, beautiful, and at ease. Founded in the heart of Colombo, we set out to create a space that blends expert craftsmanship with a warm, welcoming atmosphere — a place you look forward to visiting, not just a quick stop.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted destination for hair styling, skincare, and bridal beauty. Our team of passionate stylists stay ahead of the latest trends while honouring the personal style of every client who walks through our doors.
