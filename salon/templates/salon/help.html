Frequently Asked Questions

You can book an appointment directly through our website by clicking the Book Now button in the navigation bar. Select your preferred service, choose a date and time, and confirm your booking. You will need to be logged in to complete a booking, creating an account is free and takes under a minute.
Yes. We ask that you reschedule or cancel at least 24 hours before your appointment so our stylists can plan their schedules. To make changes, log in to your account and visit your booking history, or contact us directly by phone or email.
Yes, a free account is required to book online. This lets you view your upcoming appointments, track your booking history, and receive confirmation messages. You can sign up quickly on our Sign Up page.
We offer a range of beauty services including Hair Styling (cuts, colouring, and treatments), Facials (relaxing skin-care treatments), and Bridal Makeup (full packages tailored for your special day). We are always expanding our offerings, check our homepage for the latest.
Duration varies by service. A standard haircut typically takes 30–60 minutes, while facials run around 45–60 minutes. Bridal makeup packages can take 2–4 hours depending on the look. We recommend arriving 5–10 minutes early so we can get started on time.
Absolutely. Our bridal packages are fully customisable and include a pre-wedding consultation to discuss your vision, a trial session, and full makeup on the day. We recommend booking bridal services at least 4–6 weeks in advance to secure your date.
We accept cash, major credit and debit cards, and mobile payment options. Payment is collected at the salon after your service is complete. For bridal packages, a deposit may be required at the time of booking to hold your date.
We do not charge a cancellation fee for appointments cancelled with more than 24 hours' notice. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a small fee at our discretion. We appreciate your understanding, as last-minute cancellations affect our team's schedule.
We are located in Colombo, Sri Lanka. For the exact address and directions, please contact us by phone or email and we will be happy to guide you.
We are open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM, and on Sundays from 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM. We recommend booking ahead to guarantee your preferred time slot, especially on weekends.
